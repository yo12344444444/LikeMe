Welcome to LikeMe!
LikeMe is a revolutionary way to share and monetize content across platforms. With our floating heart button, you can easily share any content while adding value through tips and locks.
Smart Heart Button
The heart button follows you around as you browse your favorite platforms like YouTube, GitHub, X (Twitter) and more. It intelligently fades in when you're near shareable content, making it effortless to share anything that catches your eye.
Share
Quick Sharing & Integration
Click the heart when it's active to instantly copy the content's URL. The URL is automatically shared to Hodlocker and Treechat, where you can add tips or locks. Want more? Visit LikeMe's advanced settings to connect your favorite Bitcoin apps and expand your sharing capabilities.
Share
Tips & Locks: Two Ways to Engage
💝 Tips: Support Creators Tips are a direct way to support creators you love. Most of your tip goes straight to the original content creator. The best part? If the creator doesn't claim their tip, it automatically returns to you!
🔒 Locks: Boost Visibility Locks work differently - they're your way to boost content visibility in rankings. The more you lock, the more visible the content becomes. Don't worry about your funds; your locked amount returns to you after a set number of blocks. Want content to be seen more? Lock more!
Remember: Whether you tip or lock, your funds always find their way back to you if unused.
Wallet-less Tipping
🎯 Tip Anyone, Anywhere Want to support a creator but not sure if they have a wallet? No problem!

Here's how it works:

  1. Just hover over their username or content and click the heart and add a tip
  2. They can claim their tips by authenticating their identity
  3. If they don't claim it, you get your tip back
Your funds are always safe - either supporting creators or returning to you!
